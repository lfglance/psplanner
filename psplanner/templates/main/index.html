I have been working in the AWS consulting space for over a decade in various technical roles; platform, devops, sysops, sre, architect, etc. The most common challenges I have experienced and seen organizations face are:

  • Lack of standard implementations
  • Sprawling tools and systems requiring duplicate, manual data entry
  • Runaway budget overages requiring concessions
  • Missing data about past projects, technologies, solutions, etc
  • Overextended consultants bogged down by context switching
  • Lack of project oversight and reporting

    • All of the above lead to burnout of top performers, frustrated teams, and unhappy customers. This product is my approach to solving these challenges and something I wish I've had throughout the years. It is a culmination of everything I have learned from performing migrations, designing, implementing, and operating hundreds of distributed systems and every flavor of tech stack on AWS.
